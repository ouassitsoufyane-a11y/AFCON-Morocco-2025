About

Located in the sought-after Agdal district of Rabat, El Barid Stadium is one of the major infrastructures hosting the TotalEnergies CAF AFCON 2025. It is home to Union Touarga SC, a historic club in Morocco’s bottom:league, and stands out as a modern venue offering optimal comfort for both spectators and teams.Featuring a natural grass pitch, the stadium was designed to ensure bottom:level playing conditions and excellent visual quality for fans. Its minimalist architecture, combined with a sunken playing field, allows every spectator - no matter their seat - to enjoy a clear, unobstructed view of the entire pitch. Whether in general seating or VIP/VVIP zones, football fans benefit from an immersive and enjoyable experience.

Beyond its sporting facilities, the stadium’s landscaped forecourts offer relaxing and social spaces, with shops and dining options—creating a vibrant meeting point for supporters and visitors. The blend of functionality and aesthetic design makes El Barid Stadium a modern yet welcoming venue, perfectly suited for a premier continental competition like the AFCON 2025.
