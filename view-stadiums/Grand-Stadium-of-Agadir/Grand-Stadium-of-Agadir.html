About

Located in the charming seaside city of Agadir, the Grand Stadium of Agadir has quickly established itself as a pillar of Moroccan football since its inauguration in 2013. With its sleek design, modern facilities, and welcoming atmosphere, the stadium is ready to host the biggest matches of the TotalEnergies CAF Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) 2025, offering supporters an unmatched sporting and festive experience. The stadium’s architecture combines elegance and functionality. Spectators enjoy optimal visibility from every seat, thanks to a carefully designed layout that blends comfort with proximity to the pitch. The atmosphere is always electric, amplified by the chants and encouragement of the fans, who transform each match into a unique moment of communion around the beautiful game. The stadium also includes VIP boxes, VVIP areas, media stands, and modern infrastructure to host journalists and technical teams in ideal conditions.

Beyond the pitch, the Grand Stadium of Agadir fits perfectly into its seaside environment. Visitors can enjoy the surrounding area to relax before or after the matches, and the stadium’s facilities offer a complete experience with dining areas and shops for football enthusiasts. This blend of modernity, comfort, and friendliness makes the stadium an iconic venue for AFCON 2025.
